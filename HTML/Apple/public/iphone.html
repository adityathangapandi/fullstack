Iphone

The iPhone is a line of smartphones designed and marketed by Apple Inc. It was first introduced by Steve Jobs, then Apple's CEO, in January 2007 and has since become one of the most popular and influential smartphone brands worldwide The iPhone is known for its sleek and minimalist design, often characterized by its aluminum or glass enclosure. Apple emphasizes the use of high-quality materials and precise manufacturing to ensure a premium look and feel
